The MidKnight Magic
Team 9042
_














We are FTC team 9042 - The MidKnight Magic, located in West Windsor and Plainsboro, New Jersey. We are beginning our fourth year as a team. We consist of 15 students that range from 7th to 11th grade going to middle schools and high schools of the West Windsor-Plainsboro Regional School District. We are here to have fun, build some cool robots, and spread the message of STEM and FIRST throughout our community. On our website you can find information about our team, as well as our progress going through this season. You can find what we are doing as outreach into the community, as well as seeing pictures of us enjoying ourselves in this year's season for FTC FIRST Relic Recovery!
